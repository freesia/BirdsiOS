Great Horned Owl

The Great Horned Owl is a fierce nighttime hunter that preys upon a variety of mammals including skunks, rabbits, and mice. It is armed with powerful claws (talons), excellent night vision, enhanced hearing capabilities, and special feathers which allow this predator to silently attack prey. To learn more about this fascinating bird please visit the Great Horned Owl display in the Nature Center’s main hallway. Call: Great Horned Owls can be distinguished from other large owls in this area by their classic Hoo-Hoo Hooooo call. These calls can often be heard here at the Nature Center during winter which is when they are breeding.
