Carolina Wren

The Carolina Wren is a small, highly agile brown bird that frequents thickets and brush piles. This song bird can be identified by its prominent white eye stripe and its tail which is often held high while perching. A Wren’s diet is varied and consists of insects, including beetles, caterpillars, moths, crickets, bees, and ants which it finds on trees and in leaf litter. Call: A clear, melodious teakettle teakettle teakettle.
